Egypt: Measuring Eternity in Stone

c. 3100 BCE – 30 BCE

An investigation into the concepts of cosmic order, absolute balance, and the monumental pursuit of permanence along the Nile River valley.

The Principle of Ma'at

Egyptian civilization survived for millennia due to an unyielding structural worldview centered on cosmic order, balance, and justice. This entire exhibit focuses on how art, monumental construction, and everyday writing items served a singular metaphysical goal: anchoring human life to the unchanging patterns of the cosmos.

The Rosetta Stone

Granodiorite Stela

The structural operational key to decoding ancient Egyptian hieroglyphic text, preserving a royal administrative decree across three distinct written linguistic systems.
